The Sovereign AI Compliance Operating System

Sheria AI is a sovereign compliance platform designed to help regulated organizations operationalize Kenya's Artificial Intelligence Bill, 2026. Hosted on Servernah Cloud, it provides automated risk classification, continuous auditing, and audit-ready workflows.

1. Discovery Engine – Shadow AI Detection

Continuous background scans of network traffic and software inventories to reveal unmonitored third-party AI tools actively processing corporate data.

2. Kiwango Risk Engine – Statutory Classification

Automated, clause-by-clause evaluation that maps your ML deployments strictly to the four statutory risk tiers defined by the AI Commissioner.

3. Ukaguzi Audit Engine – Continuous Verification

Real-time monitoring of model drift, algorithmic bias, and demographic parity. Automatically logs system behavior to an immutable ledger for regulatory compliance.

4. Mshauri Copilot – Proactive AI Advisory

A conversational compliance assistant trained on the AI Bill 2026 and Data Protection Act 2019 to guide developers and risk managers in plain English and Swahili.
