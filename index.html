Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Scire Research — from our process to our vision.

What types of research do you handle? −

We handle market research, data analysis, M&E for NGOs, medical and pharmaceutical research, AI fact-checking, and professional training. Our team has deep expertise across healthcare, agriculture, finance, and international development.

Who do you work with? +

We work with NGOs, businesses, government agencies, academic institutions, and development partners across Kenya and internationally. Recent partners include UNICEF, Kenya's Ministry of Health, and leading businesses across East Africa.

What does the process look like? +

We follow a transparent 5-step process: Consult & Define (understand your needs), Proposal & Plan (scope and timeline), Collect & Verify (field research and data collection), Analyze & Interpret (rigorous analysis), and Report & Recommend (clear, actionable insights).

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines vary depending on scope. A market research study might take 2–4 weeks, while a comprehensive M&E evaluation could take 3–6 months. We provide a clear timeline in our proposal before we start.

How much do your services cost? +

We provide custom quotes based on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer competitive rates and can work within your budget. Contact us for a free initial consultation and quote.

Who will be working on my project? +

Every project is led by our experienced research team, with direct oversight from our founders. We assemble the right mix of skills — from field researchers to data analysts — based on your specific needs.

How do you ensure data quality? +

We follow rigorous quality assurance protocols at every stage — from field data collection (using verified tools like ODK and Kobo Toolbox) to analysis (using R, Python, and STATA). Our team is GCP certified, and we have a proven track record of reducing data errors by over 30%.

How do you ensure data confidentiality? +

We are GCP certified and follow strict data protection protocols. All client data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality. Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) are available upon request.

Can you work with us remotely? +

Yes. While we are based in Nairobi and conduct fieldwork across Kenya, we work with clients remotely through virtual meetings, email, and secure file sharing. We've successfully partnered with organizations across East Africa and internationally.

What sectors do you specialize in? +

We have deep expertise in healthcare, agriculture, finance, education, and international development. Our team has worked with Kenya's Ministry of Health (NASCOP), UNICEF, and leading businesses across these sectors.

Can you share examples of your work? +

Yes. We have case studies and testimonials from our work with UNICEF, Kenya's Ministry of Health, and other clients. Contact us and we'll share relevant examples based on your sector and research needs.

Do you offer AI services? +

Yes. Beyond traditional research, we offer AI fact-checking, proprietary dataset development, and data science consulting. We're also building Africa's verified data infrastructure and an AI research intelligence platform. Learn more on our Innovation Hub page.
